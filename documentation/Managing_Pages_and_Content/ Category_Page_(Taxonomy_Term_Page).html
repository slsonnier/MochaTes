Category Page (Taxonomy Term Page)

Saturday, March 18, 2017 4:21 AM

Just like the front page, TheMAG contains several predefined variants of taxonomy term pages.
To use some of the predefined variants, do the following:

Step 1: Go to Structure > Pages.

Step 2: Click Edit to make changes.

 

Step 3: Click the tab of the varian you would like to use and select Selection rules.

Step 4: Remove the rule “PHP Code” 

Step 5: Disable the options you do not use.
